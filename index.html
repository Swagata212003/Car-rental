Yamaha R15 V4-500rs/hr

The Yamaha R15 V4 is powered by a 155cc liquid-cooled, SOHC, single-cylinder engine featuring Variable Valve Actuation (VVA), producing 18.6 PS of power and 14.1 Nm of torque, paired with a 6-speed gearbox. It showcases an aggressive design with dual LED headlamps, an aerodynamic fairing, and sporty graphics. The bike is equipped with a digital LCD instrument cluster, Bluetooth connectivity, and multiple ride modes for a versatile riding experience. Safety features include dual-channel ABS, a slipper clutch, and a delta box frame, providing enhanced stability and control.

TVS Apache RR 310-700rs/hr

The TVS Apache RR 310 is powered by a 312.2cc liquid-cooled single-cylinder engine, producing 34 PS of power and 27.3 Nm of torque, coupled with a 6-speed gearbox and slipper clutch for smooth gear shifts. It boasts aggressive, aerodynamic styling with dual LED projector headlamps and signature DRL, offering a striking presence on the road. The motorcycle is equipped with advanced technology, including a 5-inch TFT display with Bluetooth connectivity and multiple ride modes for a customizable riding experience. Safety features include dual-channel ABS, glide-through technology, and a lightweight trellis frame that enhances stability and control.

Bajaj Pulsar-400rs/hr

The Bajaj Pulsar 200cc features a 199.5cc liquid-cooled, single-cylinder engine with triple spark technology, producing 24.5 PS of power and 18.5 Nm of torque, and is paired with a 6-speed gearbox for smooth acceleration. It boasts sporty and aggressive styling with sharp lines, LED headlamps, and split seats. The motorcycle is equipped with a digital instrument cluster and LED lighting for modern functionality. Safety is enhanced with dual-channel ABS and a perimeter frame, which provides improved handling and stability.
